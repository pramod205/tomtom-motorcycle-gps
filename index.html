TomTom Motorcycle GPS Map Update

Your TomTom motorcycle GPS device helps you navigate winding roads and scenic routes with ease. Keeping it updated ensures you always ride with the most accurate maps, route changes, speed limit alerts, and rider-specific navigation features.

Why You Should Update Your TomTom Motorcycle GPS

  • Access new scenic routes and biker-friendly paths
  • Get updated road conditions and speed zones
  • Stay on top of traffic and safety alerts
  • Enhance the performance and stability of your GPS

Things You'll Need

  • TomTom Rider or motorcycle GPS device
  • USB cable to connect your device
  • Computer with internet access
  • TomTom MyDrive Connect software

Steps to Update Maps on Your Motorcycle GPS

  • Install TomTom MyDrive Connect on your PC or Mac
  • Connect your device via USB
  • Sign in using your TomTom account
  • Check for available map or software updates
  • Click “Update” to install them
  • Wait for installation to finish before disconnecting

Troubleshooting Update Problems

  • Ensure the device battery is charged before updating
  • Restart your computer and TomTom device if the update fails
  • Use a stable Wi-Fi or ethernet internet connection
  • Disable VPN or firewall temporarily if MyDrive Connect doesn’t detect your GPS

Riding with outdated maps can lead to missed turns or closed routes. Update your TomTom Motorcycle GPS regularly for a smoother, safer ride through every adventure.
